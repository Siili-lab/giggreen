Simple by design

Made for real life in Kenya

No app to download. No CV to write. Designed for someone at a cyber cafe in Githurai.

01

Register once

Visit any cyber cafe. Enter your phone number and verify with an SMS code. Pick your green skills. Done in under 10 minutes.

02

Get SMS alerts

When a gig matches your skills and location, you get an SMS instantly. Reply YES to apply — no internet needed.

03

Get paid to M-Pesa

Complete the gig, mark it done via USSD on any phone. Payment releases straight to your M-Pesa. No waiting. No paperwork.
